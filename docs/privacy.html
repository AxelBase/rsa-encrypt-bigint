Privacy Policy

Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to the Simple RSA Encrypt with BigInt web utility, a purely educational, client-side demonstration tool for RSA encryption using JavaScript BigInt. This Privacy Policy explains our practices regarding any information related to your use of the tool. Because the application runs entirely in your browser and is stateless with no backend, we collect, store, transmit, or process zero personal data or user content. This policy is provided for transparency and to affirm our commitment to maximum privacy.

2. Information We Do Not Collect

We do not collect any information from you, including:

  • Personal Identifiable Information — names, email addresses, IP addresses, or any identifiers
  • Usage or Technical Data — browser type, device details, operating system, or timestamps
  • Input Content — plaintext messages, generated keys, ciphertexts, or any numbers you enter or produce
  • Analytics or Tracking Data — no cookies, pixels, beacons, or third-party analytics scripts are used

All operations — key generation, encryption, and display — occur exclusively on your device. Nothing is ever sent to us or any third party.

3. How the Tool Operates

When you use this utility:

  • All RSA mathematics and BigInt computations happen locally via JavaScript
  • No server requests are made at any point
  • Browser temporary memory may hold your inputs during the session, but this is cleared on page refresh or close
  • Any copy-to-clipboard or manual saving of keys/ciphertext is handled by your browser only

This design guarantees complete data isolation and makes the tool suitable for educational experimentation without privacy concerns.

4. Third-Party Services and CDNs

The site uses Bootstrap and Bootstrap Icons via public CDNs for styling. These CDNs may log standard HTTP request metadata (e.g., IP address) when fetching resources, but we have no control over or access to that data. No tracking scripts or analytics are embedded.

5. Cookies and Similar Technologies

We do not use cookies, localStorage, sessionStorage, or any persistent client-side storage for tracking purposes. The application remains fully stateless.

6. Security Disclaimer

While we collect no data, this is not a production cryptographic tool. It uses weak randomness (Math.random) and textbook RSA without padding. We recommend using secure, audited libraries for any real encryption needs. Security of your device and browser is your responsibility.

7. Children's Privacy

The tool is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will appear here with a new effective date. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.

This utility is built on a zero-data-collection model. Your privacy is protected by design — thank you for using it responsibly for educational purposes only.
